# 🌍 K.F.T – Social Welfare Organization Website

Welcome to the official website project of **K.F.T (Khidmat Foundation Trust)** – a community-driven social welfare organization. Our mission is to support underprivileged families through food distribution, educational support, financial aid, and emergency relief.

## ✨ Features

- 🏠 Homepage with mission, updates, and donation links
- 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 About Us – learn about K.F.T's vision and team
- 📝 Aid Application Form (Google Forms integrated)
- 💸 Online donation section (bank & payment methods)
- 📷 Event Gallery showcasing past distributions and support drives
- 📊 Transparency through reports and financial breakdowns
- 📞 Contact page with map, WhatsApp, and email links
- 🔐 Admin-ready backend structure (optional)
